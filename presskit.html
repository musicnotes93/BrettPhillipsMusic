Brett Phillips is a multi-instrumentalist, singer/songwriter, composer, and music educator. Growing up in North Carolina, he began his introduction to music at a young age through piano lessons before discovering a love of the guitar. Starting at age 15, he started performing professionally and teaching lessons from his home, before going to college for Music Performance and Music Education at Campbell University.


He has released 3 albums with his band The Separate, along with recording music for online content which garnered millions of views, and has taught hundreds of students through various music programs.


Currently, he is active in performing as a session musician locally, writing and recording original music.
